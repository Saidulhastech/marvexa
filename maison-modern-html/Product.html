Product Description

The Linen Relaxed Blazer redefines effortless sophistication. Cut from 100% premium European linen, this piece moves beautifully between structured office settings and relaxed weekend occasions. The relaxed silhouette is intentionally oversized through the shoulder and tapering gently at the hip — flattering without trying.

Single-button fastening, notched lapels, and patch pockets keep the design clean and purposeful. The natural texture of the linen gives each piece a subtle, organic character that gets better with wear.

  • 100% European Premium Linen — breathable and temperature-regulating
  • Relaxed oversized fit, sizes run true to size
  • Single-button closure with horn-effect button
  • Two front patch pockets, one chest pocket
  • Partially lined with lightweight cupro for comfort
  • Dry clean recommended; gentle hand wash acceptable
Materials & Care
  • Shell: 100% European Linen (OEKO-TEX® certified)
  • Lining: 60% Cupro, 40% Viscose
  • Buttons: Natural Horn
  • Dry clean for best results; cold hand wash with mild detergent
  • Do not bleach — lay flat to dry, reshape while damp
  • Warm iron on reverse side to preserve texture
