# Modern Fashion E-commerce Homepage — Project Instructions

> This file contains the complete design brief and system instructions for building the Modern Fashion E-commerce Homepage. All design decisions must follow these guidelines.

---

## Project Goal

Design a **Modern Fashion E-commerce Homepage** with a clean, stylish, functional, conversion-focused, and premium e-commerce feel.

The design should feel contemporary, polished, minimal, and easy to shop. It should balance strong fashion visuals with practical e-commerce functionality.

**Avoid:** too luxury, too editorial, too magazine-like, too playful, too streetwear-heavy, or too generic.

**Target feeling:** Modern + Refined + Highly Usable + Conversion-Focused

---

## 1. Section Plan (in order)

1. **Hero Section** — Clean modern hero with strong fashion image, bold headline, short intro, and CTA: `Shop Collection`, `Explore Lookbook`.
2. **Brand Intro / Philosophy** — Short brand message with simple modern layout focused on quality, comfort, style, and everyday confidence.
3. **Featured Categories** — Clear category cards for Women, Men, Bags, Accessories, New Arrivals with modern image layout.
4. **New Arrivals / Featured Products** — Modern product grid with image, product name, price, color swatches, wishlist, and quick view/add to cart hover.
5. **Lookbook / Shop the Look** — Clean lifestyle outfit showcase with product hotspots and `Shop the Look` CTA.
6. **Best Sellers** — Popular products section with conversion-friendly product cards.
7. **Craftsmanship / Materials** — Modern quality section highlighting materials, durability, comfort, and thoughtful design.
8. **Campaign Banner** — Full-width modern campaign banner with strong image, short headline, and CTA.
9. **Testimonials / Press Mentions** — Clean testimonial or press mention section with subtle social proof.
10. **Blog Journal** — Modern article cards for style tips, wardrobe guides, care tips, or brand stories.
11. **Newsletter CTA** — Clean email signup section with modern brand messaging.
12. **Footer** — Organized footer with shop links, customer care, about, social links, policies, and newsletter.

## 2. Typography Style Guide

### Font Pairing
- **Heading Font:** `Plus Jakarta Sans`
- **Body / UI Font:** `Inter`

Use `Plus Jakarta Sans` for headings — stylish, modern, clean. Use `Inter` for body text, product info, navigation, buttons, forms, and footer.

Typography should feel: clean, modern, confident, readable, and e-commerce friendly.

---

## 4. Modern Design Rules

- Create a **modern fashion e-commerce homepage**, not luxury/editorial/magazine.
- Use clean grids, strong spacing, and polished product presentation.
- Keep the design minimal but not empty.
- Use modern visual hierarchy with bold headings and clear CTAs.
- Make product discovery fast and easy.
- Keep sections highly usable and conversion-focused.
- Product sections **must include**: product name, price, color swatches, wishlist, and quick view/add to cart hover.
- Use clean cards, subtle borders, soft backgrounds, modern hover states, and clear buttons.
- Use lifestyle imagery with modern cropping and balanced whitespace.

### Avoid:
- Overly decorative serif typography
- Magazine / artistic editorial compositions
- Heavy shadows
- Loud colors
- Playful rounded cards
- Discount-heavy design
