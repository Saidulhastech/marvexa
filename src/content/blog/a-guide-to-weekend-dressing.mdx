## Comfort Without the Compromise

Relaxed and sloppy are not the same thing. The weekend uniform that actually works keeps the comfort of soft, easy pieces while holding on to a little structure — so you look like you chose the outfit rather than surrendered to it.

## The Formula

Start with one relaxed piece and one with shape. Soft drawstring trousers with a clean knit. An easy tee under a structured overshirt. The structured element does the heavy lifting; the relaxed one keeps it from feeling like a workday.

## Elevate with Details

Weekend polish lives in the small things: a leather sneaker instead of a trainer, a knit instead of a hoodie, a belt that pulls the look together. None of it sacrifices comfort, and all of it reads as effort.

## Keep the Palette Quiet

Saturdays are not the day for your loudest pieces. Keep the colours calm and tonal and the whole look settles into something easy and considered — exactly the impression weekend dressing should give.
